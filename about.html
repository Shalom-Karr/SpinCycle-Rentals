About Us

"We work hard to earn your trust, not just your business."

Our Story

SpinCycle Rentals was founded by two friends, Adam and Bruce, who were frustrated by the time and money wasted at laundromats. We understood firsthand the stress of having to pack up heavy bags of clothes, find quarters, and wait for hours in a crowded facility.

At the same time, we recognized that buying and maintaining your own expensive laundry machines isn't feasible for everyone. It involves high upfront costs, heavy lifting, and the constant fear of expensive breakdowns.

We created SpinCycle to bring a simpler, more affordable, and completely trustworthy alternative to Cleveland residents. Our mission is to bring the convenience of in-home laundry to everyone without the traditional burdens of ownership.

Our Core Values

We are a small, local business that treats every customer like a neighbor, not just a number.

Trust & Transparency

We know you're placing your home and comfort in our hands. We earn that trust through open communication, clear agreements, and a strict "no surprises" policy. No hidden fees, no fine print.

Reliability

Laundry is essential to daily life. All our machines are carefully inspected and tested to meet factory standards. If an issue arises, we guarantee repairs and service at no extra charge.

Personal Customer Care

We prioritize clear answers and step-by-step guidance through the rental process. When you call or email us, you're speaking to someone locally who genuinely cares about your experience.

White-Glove Service

The "Simple Promise": We handle the machines so you can handle life. We manage the delivery, professional installation, and testing to ensure everything is running perfectly before we leave.
