The All-In-One Advantage

What exactly is an all-in-one washer/dryer? It's exactly what it sounds like: a single, high-quality machine that washes and dries your clothes in the exact same drum.

  • Set It & Forget It

    Put dirty clothes in before work, come home to clean, dry clothes. No more transferring heavy, wet laundry from a washer to a dryer.

  • Ventless Technology

    No dryer exhaust vent? No problem. Our modern machines use condensation drying, meaning they don't need to vent hot air outside.

  • Standard 110V Outlet

    You don't need a heavy-duty 220V outlet. Our machines plug right into any standard three-prong household electrical outlet.

  • Full Size (2.7 cu ft)

    This isn't a tiny, plastic countertop toy. It's a real, heavy-duty appliance designed for everyday apartment laundry.
